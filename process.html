Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC):

It is a process we us to design, develop and test high quality software. Egal use this process to produce high-quality software that meets customer expectations, reaches completion within times and cost estimates. It consists of a detailed plan outlining how specific software can be created, handled, replaced and upgraded. The life cycle defines a methodology for optimizing the efficiency of applications and overall development process.

Agile Software Development Life Cycle:

We use Agile SDLC methodology because we believe that every project needs to be handled differently and the existing methods need to be tailored to best suit the project requirements. In Agile, the tasks are divided into time small time frames to deliver specific features for a release. We assure you get a flexible approach to planning, development through a constantly modified prototype, regular status reports, and a quick response from us by using agile product development if scenarios change quickly, such as suggestions from you that certain functionality is not quite correct. This all guarantees that we perform on schedule and on budget for your project.
